Residential & Commercial Concrete

From decorative patios to heavy-duty industrial foundations, Virgillo & Nicholson delivers high-quality concrete work that stands the test of time. We have extensive experience pouring, finishing, and repairing concrete structures across Western Pennsylvania.

Whether you need a new driveway or a reinforced floor for a steel building, we have the team and equipment to handle the job.

Our Concrete Capabilities

We specialize in a wide range of flatwork and structural concrete applications:

  • Driveways & Sidewalks: Durable surfaces designed to handle Pennsylvania weather.
  • Garage & Steel Building Floors: Smooth, level, and reinforced for heavy loads.
  • Pads & Footers: Precision pouring for sheds, HVAC units, and structural support.
  • Foundation Block Work: Solid foundations for new homes and additions.
  • Patios & Porches: Beautiful outdoor living spaces.
  • Pool Decks: Safe, slip-resistant surrounds for your pool.
  • Driveway Repairs: Fixing cracks and damage to extend the life of your driveway.
  • And More: Custom concrete solutions for any project.
