About Us

Family Owned & Fully Insured

Virgillo & Nicholson Construction LLC is a fully insured, family-owned construction company based in Vanderbilt, Pennsylvania, specializing in both residential and commercial projects. We incorporate 45 years of industry experience following our acquisition of Tony R. Huffine Excavating LLC.
