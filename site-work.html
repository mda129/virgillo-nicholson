Precision Site Preparation

At Virgillo & Nicholson Construction, we understand that a solid project starts with the ground up. Whether you are prepping for a new home foundation or clearing a commercial lot, we have the heavy equipment and the experience to get it done right.

Following our acquisition of Tony R. Huffine Excavating, we bring over 45 years of excavation expertise to every job site in Western Pennsylvania.

What We Do

We handle all aspects of earthwork and site management. No job is too large or too messy for our team.

  • Site prep for new homes
  • Excavation for footers & foundations
  • Property clearing & Stump removal
  • Driveway & Dirt removal
  • Parking lots & Cement pads
  • Drainage solutions

Modern Technology

Precision matters when you are pouring foundations or grading a driveway.

We use modern, calibrated laser levels for all our site work. This ensures perfect grading for water drainage and level foundations every single time.
